Common Name(s) Scientific Name Identification Possible Confusion Taste/Smell Habitat
Dryad's Saddle, Pheasants Back Polyporus squamosus Large bracket fungus, Offwhite/ochre with brown concentric scales, white flesh, white sport print Low to none Smells like watermelon or cucumber when young Deciduous trees or stumps, particularly Manitoba Maple
American Yellow Fly Agaric Amanita muscaria var. Guessowii Yellow to Orange cap with numerous white warts Amanita muscaria var. flavivolvata, Amanita muscaria var. muscaria, Amanita muscaria var. persicina Warning: contains acid/poison Hardwood and Conifer forests, found on the ground sometimes single, scattered or in fairy rings
Giant Puffball Calvatia gigantia Round, solid white flesh, no stem Must have no visible gills, solid white like mozzarella very mushroomy Nutrient rich woodlands, found on the ground
Flowery Blewit Lepista irina white to blush cap, undulating margin, narrow crowded gills, creamy white sport print The Clouded Agaric has more grey colouration and lacks the floral scent floral odour, please note the flowery blewit can cause stomach issues if not properly prepared Grows low to the ground, primarily deciduous but sometimes conifer forests
